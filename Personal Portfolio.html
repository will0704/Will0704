Mix n' Match

Mix 'n' Match Card Game is an engaging and dynamic card game where players aim to create matching pairs from a randomized deck. Each card features unique elements, and the goal is to draw and pair these cards based on matching characteristics, such as images, symbols, or themes. As cards are revealed and matched, players must use their memory and strategy to find and pair the most combinations. With its fast-paced, ever-changing gameplay, Mix 'n' Match offers endless fun and challenges for players of all ages.

PuTask

PuTask is a specialized task management app designed to support individuals with ADHD. It offers a user-friendly interface that helps users break down tasks into manageable steps, set clear priorities, and stay organized. Features include customizable reminders, visual task tracking, and a focus mode to minimize distractions. PuTask also provides tools for setting goals, tracking progress, and maintaining motivation, making it easier for users to stay on top of their responsibilities and achieve their objectives. Whether for daily tasks, long-term projects, or personal goals, PuTask is tailored to enhance productivity and manage ADHD effectively.
